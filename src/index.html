Tips & Tricks

{

}

Each bakery you ever visit has a different atmosphere and environment. Here are some tips to make the most of your trip to Tous Les Jours!

  • Go With Friends... There's nothing better than a trip to a cafe ~ than a trip to a cafe with friends! Enjoy a variety of drinks and pastries over priceless hours of conversation.
  • Try New Things Each Time... The TLJ menu is bigger than you think. Why not change up your routine and try something new? Try something sweet on one day and something savory on another day!
  • Get Cozy... Don't stay in on a rainy day. Put on your favorite cozy clothes, grab your favorite crafts or books, and head over to TLJ to experience the best place to both relax and study.
  • Get More Than One... Don't just get one pastry and be done for the day. Pastries and breads are meant to be eaten with a tasty drink. Get a croissant and a coffee. Or cake and an iced tea. The world is your oyster!
  • Ask Around... Don't know what to get? Ask a worker! They're always super friendly, and know the menu better than anyone. Ask for a recommendation and you won't be disappointed.
